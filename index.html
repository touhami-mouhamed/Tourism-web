East Nusa Tenggara

Come on and let's discover such a massive ISLAND

It is an Indonesian province located in the eastern part of the Lesser Sunda Islands, comprising over 500 islands, the most prominent being Flores, Sumba, and West Timor. Its capital is Kupang. The province is famous for Komodo National Park (home to Komodo dragons), the colorful lakes of Mount Kelimutu, and its unique beaches. It is characterized by its rugged terrain and a predominantly Catholic population.
